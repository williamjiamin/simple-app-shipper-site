Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

Overview

Simple App Shipper is a macOS application that helps developers manage their App Store Connect workflows. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Simple App Shipper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. The app runs entirely on your Mac.

App Store Connect Credentials

Your App Store Connect API credentials (Key ID, Issuer ID, and Private Key) are stored locally on your Mac in the macOS Keychain. These credentials are:

  • Never transmitted to any server other than Apple's App Store Connect API (api.appstoreconnect.apple.com)
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Encrypted at rest by the macOS Keychain
  • Used solely to authenticate API requests to App Store Connect on your behalf

Network Communication

The app communicates only with:

  • Apple's App Store Connect API (api.appstoreconnect.apple.com) — to manage your apps, versions, screenshots, and other App Store data
  • Apple's iTunes Lookup API (itunes.apple.com) — to fetch app icons for display

No data is sent to any other service or analytics provider.

Local Storage

The app stores preferences (selected theme, window position) in macOS UserDefaults. Your safeguard password hash is stored locally and never transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Simple App Shipper does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

This app is a professional developer tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
